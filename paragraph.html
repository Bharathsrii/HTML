vampier diaries

The lives, loves, dangers and disasters in the town, Mystic Falls, Virginia. Creatures of unspeakable horror lurk beneath this town as a teenage girl is suddenly torn between two vampire brothers. After centuries of quarreling, Stefan and Damon Salvatore return to their original town of Mystic Fall, Virginia.


the originals

The Originals centers on the Mikealson siblings. The Mikealsons return to New Orleans to get back the city they helped build. When Klaus founds out that Hayley is expecting a child, he will do anything to protect his unborn child against his enemies. "Always and forever".


the legacies

Premise. Legacies follows Hope Mikaelson, the daughter of Klaus Mikaelson and Hayley Marshall, who is descended from some of the most powerful vampire, werewolf, and witch bloodlines. Two years after the events of The Originals, 17-year-old Hope attends the Salvatore School for the Young and Gifted.
